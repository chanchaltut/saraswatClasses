Our Story

Built on a foundation of
knowledge & integrity

Saraswat Classes was established with a singular mission — to make quality education accessible and transformative. Based in Sonepur, Odisha, co-founded by Md Santadeepa Hota & Siddharth Swarup Tripathy, bringing together decades of teaching expertise under one roof.

We serve students from Class 6 to 12 across CBSE, CHSE, and Odia Medium boards, with specialized coaching in Physics for 11th & 12th graders. Our residential institute at Patabhadi, Sonepur provides a holistic academic environment.
